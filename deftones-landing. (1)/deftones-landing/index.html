About The Band

Deftones is an American alternative metal band formed in Sacramento, California, in 1988. The band consists of Chino Moreno (lead vocals, guitar), Stephen Carpenter (lead guitar), Abe Cunningham (drums), and Frank Delgado (keyboards, turntables).

They started as a nu metal band but evolved into something more complex and experimental, blending heavy riffs with dreamy atmospheres. Their sound has been described as brutal, dreamy, heavy, and sexual.

After Chi Cheng's tragic accident in 2008 and passing in 2013, Sergio Vega joined on bass until 2021. Their latest album "Private Music" was released in August 2025 to critical acclaim.
